What you get

Everything a music player should be

✦

Stunning interface

Live starfield, real-time audio visualizer, glassy panels. A music player you actually want to look at.

◎

Smart library

Instant search, 10 auto-detected genre buckets, sort by title, artist, album, or most played.

≡

Playlists

Create playlists, drag and drop songs in, right-click to add from anywhere. Your music, your way.

♦

Every format

MP3, FLAC, WMA, AAC, OGG, WAV, M4A. If you own it, Aria plays it.

♥

Liked songs & history

Heart your favorites. Track your most-played tracks. Your taste, remembered across sessions.

◻

Works on any device

Runs at localhost:5000. Open it on your phone, tablet, or any computer on your home network.

Setup

Running in under a minute

1

Download & unzip

Grab the latest release from GitHub. Unzip it anywhere on your machine.

2

Add your music

Drop your MP3, FLAC, WMA — any audio files — into the Aria folder.

3

Open Aria.exe

Your browser opens automatically. Your full library loads, ready to play.
